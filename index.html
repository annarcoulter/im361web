Welcome to Café Meow!

Café Meow is a celebrated cat café based in St. Louis, Missouri. We serve coffee, drinks, and a wide variety of bakery items. Don't forget to pick up some treats at the counter for our furry friends!

All of our cats are available for adoption. Come down and meet your future feline!
